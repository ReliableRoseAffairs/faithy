Trust Deposit Process

To ensure safety and trust during meetups, we have implemented a trust deposit system. Here are some key points:

  • The participant deposits a certain amount of money before the meetup.
  • The deposited money is held securely by an intermediary and is not accessible by either party during the meetup.
  • After the meetup, if there are no issues, the participant confirms the successful meetup, and the money is refunded.
  • If there are any issues, a dispute can be raised, and the intermediary will review the matter.

This system ensures that the money is safe and cannot be used by the other party unless the meetup is successfully completed without any problems.
